Brief

Position :Research and Design Engineer

Sponsor : Dwight Look College of Engineering (Dr Srinvasta’s Lab – Shape Memory Research)

Industries : Product Design, Mechanical Engineering, User Experience

Need Statement :Develop a new method of construction that incorporates "beauty and elegance" into urban infrastructure .

 

Timeframe: 1 Year

Contributions!

Pitched and combined design ideas to produce a mold-maker capable of making "beautiful" curved structures with minimal cost

-Used natural material properties associated with concrete (such as its release of heat when it changes form) and smart memory polymers (SMPs) (that stiffen / loosen their form when exposed to heat) to minimize cost on the users end

-Researched concrete mixing methods and material science principles to select optimal concrete types that would release enough heat to trigger the smart-memory polymer layer beneath it to become flexible as the concrete transforms

-Communicated with a variety of students from other academic

-Produced an introduction video for Texas A-M's Engineering Virtual Video Showcase of the prototype.
