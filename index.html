About me

Software and design permeate our everyday interactions with the world, so I want to take part in making those interactions meaningful and intuitive. With a mulitidisiplinary background in engineering product development and human interaction research, I have a keen eye for perfecting details from a consumer's point of view and a solid understanding of technical restraints that come with developing products for modern consumers to help inform my design decisions. I aim to design elegant solutions that apply modern research and user-testing methods.

During my (seemingly scarce) free time, I can be seen writing screenplays for local film festivals and singing in kareoke joints with a Disney playlist. Please, don't be afriad to reach out to me to ask any questions at solade@tamu.edu .
